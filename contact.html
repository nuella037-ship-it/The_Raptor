Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common inquiries we receive.

How do I submit a press release or news tip?

You can email our newsroom directly at news@theraptor.com with the subject line “TIP: [your headline]”. For sensitive documents, we encourage using our encrypted dropbox — contact us for the link.

Can I republish an article from The Raptor?

We offer syndication licenses for other media outlets. Please reach out to our partnerships team at press@theraptor.com with details about your publication and audience size.

How can I request a correction or retraction?

We take accuracy very seriously. If you spot an error, email our editorial team at jane.doe@theraptor.com with the article headline, the error, and a correction source. We aim to resolve all requests within 2 business days.

Do you accept guest columns or op-eds?

Yes, we welcome thoughtful, fact‑based opinion pieces from subject‑matter experts. Please send a brief pitch (2–3 sentences) and your CV to our editor at jane.doe@theraptor.com. We do not publish unsolicited full drafts.

I have a confidential document to share — is there a secure way?

Absolutely. We use industry‑standard secure file transfer. Please contact our news desk at +1 (800) 555‑0199 and ask for the security team to arrange a secure channel.
