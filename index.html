Global Climate Summit Reaches Historic Agreement

By Elena Rodriguez • 5 min read • 142 comments

In a landmark turn of events, representatives from 195 nations have unanimously pledged to cut carbon emissions by 50% by 2030, marking the most ambitious climate accord in history. The deal includes a $200 billion fund to support developing nations in their green transition, strict penalties for non-compliance, and a new independent oversight committee.

  • Binding emissions targets for every signatory
  • Phased elimination of fossil fuel subsidies by 2028
  • Mandatory reforestation commitments
